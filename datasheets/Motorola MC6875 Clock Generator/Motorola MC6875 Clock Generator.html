Motorola MC6875 Data Sheet


MC6800 Clock Generator

Intended to supply the non-overlapping phase 1 and phase 2 clock signals required by the microprocessor, the clock generator is compatible with the 1.0, 1.5, and 2.0 MHz versions on the MC6800. Both the oscillator and high capacitance driver elements are included along with numerous other logic accessory functions for easy system expansion.

Schottky technology is employed for high speed and PNP-buffered inputs are employed for NMOS compatibility. A single +5 V power supply, and a crystal or RC network for frequency determination are required.
